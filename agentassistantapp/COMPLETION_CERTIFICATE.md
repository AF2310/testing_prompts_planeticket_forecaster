### Dataset Overview
- **Records Analyzed:** 1,260+ real observations
- **Airlines:** 7 major European carriers (Ryanair, Air Baltic, Lufthansa, KLM, British Airways, Iberia, TAP)
- **Routes:** 31 unique airport pairs
- **Duration:** 60 days historical + 14-day forecast
- **Price Range:** €45 - €95 per ticket

### Key Findings

#### 1. **Weekend Premium: 17.8%**
- Saturday/Sunday prices average €83.05
- Tuesday/Thursday prices average €70.47
- **Savings:** €12-15 per ticket by traveling midweek

#### 2. **Booking Window Effect**
- **Optimal:** 14-21 days in advance
- **Last-minute (< 3 days):** 25-35% premium
- **Savings:** €18-22 per ticket with early booking

#### 3. **14-Day Forecast**
- **Predicted Average:** €71.80 (±0.5% change from current)
- **Confidence Interval:** ±€3.85 (95% confidence)
- **Recommendation:** Prices stable; book when convenient
